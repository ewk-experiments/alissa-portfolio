How I Work
Principles that drive results
🧶

I deliver on ambiguous, complex projects

My approach combines my skills in design thinking coaching, stakeholder management, negotiation, storytelling, with end-to-end design skills like research and testing, wireframing, prototyping, and high-fidelity handoff.

— Lead product manager's feedback
🤝

I embrace collaboration early and often

I make sure to involve the right stakeholders (product management, engineering, front-end development, content and documentation, etc) early and often so they're involved in the process and can contribute their unique expertise and perspectives. This also reduces rework down the road!

— Software engineer's feedback
💡

I contribute back and optimize processes

I'm always looking for ways to optimize processes, whether that's internally for my team — like successfully advocating for and getting funding for better design collaboration tools — or pushing for experiences that better fit our specific users' needs, even if it deviates from overarching design system patterns.

— Manager's feedback
🧠

I enjoy learning and knowledge sharing

In addition to leveling up my own skills, I love helping others level up as well. I've presented personalized learning sessions to my design peers on accessibility, inclusive design, and negotiation skills specific to my team and context.

— Peer feedback
